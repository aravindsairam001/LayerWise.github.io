Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our 3D printing services

We accept STL, OBJ, 3MF, STEP, and IGES files. For best results, we recommend STL or 3MF formats. Our team can also work with CAD files and provide design optimization suggestions.

Standard orders are completed within 3-5 business days. Rush orders can be completed in 24-48 hours for an additional fee. Complex or large parts may require additional time, which we'll communicate upfront.

For FDM: PLA+, PETG, ABS, TPU, Wood Fill, Metal Fill, Carbon Fiber reinforced, and engineering-grade materials. For Resin: Standard, Tough, Flexible, Castable, and Biocompatible resins in various colors.
